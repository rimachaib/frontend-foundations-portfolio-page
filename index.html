About Me

After spending the last 6 years working as an environmental scientist, I began to notice, that technology was advancing the world so quickly; but, unfortunately my industry was not adapting. After this realization I felt emboldened to push my technical skill set farther by enrolling in Thinkful's Software Engineering Program with the hopes of one day developing tools that will help my industry work better.

When I'm not on my computer, learning new programming languages or furthering my personal projects, you can find me outside, rock climbing, hiking, or running around a brand new city.
